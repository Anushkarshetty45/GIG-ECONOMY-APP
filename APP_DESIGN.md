- 50+ individual stars
- Size: 2px diameter circles

- Movement: Slow float down from top to bottom
- Speed: Random 15-30 seconds per cycle
- Opacity: Fade in/out (0.3 to 1.0)
- Starting positions: Random across entire viewport
- Continuous loop

## SIDEBAR FEATURE DESCRIPTIONS (User Guide Format)

### 📊 Dashboard

**What it does:**
Your home base - shows you a quick overview of everything at a glance.

**How to use it:**
1. See your monthly income, expenses, and net income
2. View recent transactions and expenses
3. Check your savings goals progress
4. See your financial health score
5. Use quick action buttons to add income, expenses, or view analytics

**When to use:**
Every time you log in - it's your financial snapshot!

---

### 💰 Income

**What it does:**
Track all money you earn from your gigs (Uber, DoorDash, freelancing, etc.)

**How to use it:**
1. Click "Add Income" button
2. Enter:
   - Amount you earned (e.g., $150)
   - Platform (Uber, Upwork, etc.)
   - Date you earned it
   - Notes (optional)
3. Click Save
4. View all your income history in a list
5. Edit or delete entries if needed

**When to use:**
After every gig or job to keep track of your earnings!

---

### 💳 Expenses

**What it does:**
Track money you spend on business-related things (gas, equipment, food for deliveries)

**How to use it:**
1. Click "Add Expense" button
2. Enter:
   - Amount spent (e.g., $45)
   - Category (Gas, Food, Office Supplies, etc.)
   - Description (what you bought)
   - Date
   - Payment method
3. Click Save
4. View expense history
5. Filter by category or date

**When to use:**
Whenever you spend money for work - this helps with taxes!

---

### 🎯 Goals (Savings Goals)

**What it does:**
Set financial goals and track your progress (like saving for a vacation, new laptop, emergency fund)

**How to use it:**
1. Click "Create Goal" button
2. Enter:
   - Goal name (e.g., "Vacation Fund")
   - Target amount (e.g., $3,000)
   - Deadline (optional)
   - Choose icon and color
3. Add money to your goal as you save
4. Watch progress bar fill up!
5. Mark as complete when you reach target

**When to use:**
Set goals anytime, update them when you save money!

---

### 📈 Analytics

**What it does:**
Shows you charts and graphs to understand your money patterns

**How to use it:**
1. View pie charts showing:
   - Where your income comes from (which platforms)
   - What categories you spend on most
2. See line graphs of income vs expenses over time
3. Look at monthly trends

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Current Architecture:
- **Frontend:** React Native with Expo (supports Web, iOS, Android, Desktop)
- **State Management:** Zustand with AsyncStorage persistence
- **Navigation:** React Navigation (Bottom tabs + Stack navigation)
- **UI Components:** Custom glassmorphic components
- **Theming:** Dark/Light mode support
- **Data Storage:** Local-only (no API/backend integration)
